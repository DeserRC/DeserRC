## About me
I’m Diego Cruz, a Full Stack Developer with over 8 years of experience, specialized in building robust and scalable solutions for the financial market and the game industry. I work across a wide technical stack, including **Java, Kotlin, Go, Elixir, JavaScript, and TypeScript**, with frameworks such as **Spring, Angular, and React Native**.

My expertise ranges from **APIs and Microservices** development to integrating **relational databases** (SQLite, H2, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Oracle) and **NoSQL** solutions (MongoDB, Redis). I’m also proficient with messaging systems like **RabbitMQ** and **Kafka**, and CI/CD tooling including **GitHub Actions, GitLab CI/CD, and Jenkins**.

I have solid experience with **Docker, Kubernetes**, and cloud platforms such as **GCP, Azure, and AWS**, ensuring efficient deployments and reliable infrastructure. I hold a degree in **Systems Analysis and Development** from **Universidade Anhembi Morumbi**. I’m passionate about solving complex problems, combining technical depth with a practical mindset focused on delivering value, and I’m known for writing clean code and implementing innovative solutions.
